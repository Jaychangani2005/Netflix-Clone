Frequently Asked Questions
What is Netflix?
Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries and more – on thousands of internet-connected devices.

You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want, without a single ad – all for one low monthly price. There's always something new to discover, and new TV shows and movies are added every week!
How much does Netflix cost??
Netflix offers various subscription plans with different pricing tiers depending on the features and number of devices you want to use. Visit the Netflix website for the latest pricing details.
Where can I watch?
You can watch Netflix on various devices including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, gaming consoles, and more. As long as you have an internet connection, you can access Netflix content.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel your Netflix subscription at any time. Go to your account settings and follow the cancellation instructions.
What can I watch on Netflix?
Netflix offers a vast library of TV shows, movies, documentaries, anime, and more. The content catalog varies by region and is regularly updated with new releases.
Is Netflix good for kids?
Netflix provides a dedicated kids' section with family-friendly content suitable for various age groups. Parents can set up parental controls to manage what their kids can watch.
